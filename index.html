'The best little club in Billinge'

Bispham Hall Club was originally founded in 1914, with a re-development in the 1980s to update the ageing, wooden clubhouse. By having a two year development programme, this enabled the club to stay open for its loyal members. We now have over 450 members and always warmly welcome more into our family. Our lively club boasts the best in live sports, showing across 3 large TVs, great weekly entertainment and award winning cask ales. Situated in Billinge, Wigan we invite all our customers old and new to come and take advantage of all our sporting facilities which include bowling, pool, darts & dominoes.
