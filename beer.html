Our beers

Cask ales

Our selection of cask ales alternates on a weekly basis. This week we currently have Moorhouses Straw dog, Wilyfox Skylark and Bradfield Farmers Stout on cask this week. Don't forget to show your CAMRA card for 10% off our cask ales!

Other beers

We currently have Carling, San Miguel, Strongbow, Fosters, and Guinness on draft as well as a large selection of all popular spirits. Bottles of Hobgoblin and Koppaberg are also available. We also have a large selection of non-alcoholic drinks including Guinness 0% and Heineken 0%. We also have a variety of vegan and gluten free beers available.

Soft drinks

We have a large variety of soft drinks available including Schweppes lemonade, Coca-Cola, Fruit-Shoots and J2O's.
